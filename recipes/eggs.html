Eggs

Recipe Description

I love eggs more than any food of all time. I make them for all breakfasts if I can, so here's how i do it.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs (or as many as you want)
  • American cheese

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl. Use a whisk to beat these eggs like they stole your girl
  2. Pour beaten eggs onto a preheated gridle smothered in butter
  3. Take eggs off when slightly runny. Top with American cheese
